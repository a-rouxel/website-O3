O3 — Orchestrator of Orbital Operations

A comprehensive system for coordinating planning, scheduling, and execution of orbital operations. O3 enables autonomous mission management by orchestrating complex operations while satisfying temporal, resource, and physical constraints.

Architecture Concepts Updates

Keywords: orbital operations • mission planning • scheduling • constraint satisfaction • autonomy

Overview

Orbital operations require careful coordination of planning, scheduling, and execution to achieve mission objectives while respecting complex constraints. O3 provides an integrated orchestration system that automates this coordination, enabling more efficient and reliable mission operations.

Problem

Orbital operations involve complex interactions between planning, scheduling, and execution. Manual coordination is error-prone and doesn't scale. Systems need to handle temporal constraints, resource limitations, and real-time adjustments autonomously.

Approach

O3 integrates planning, scheduling, and execution into a unified orchestration system. It uses constraint-based optimization to generate feasible operation sequences and adaptive scheduling to handle dynamic conditions and resource conflicts.

Key Results

  • Automated orchestration reduces manual planning time by enabling real-time operation coordination.
  • Constraint satisfaction ensures all temporal, resource, and physical constraints are respected during execution.
  • Integrated planning and scheduling improves resource utilization and mission efficiency.
  • Adaptive execution management enables dynamic replanning in response to changing conditions.
